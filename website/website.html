SecondGlance

A browser extension that detects ads, AI-generated content and black unwanted AI features

Features

  • Detects and blocks intrusive advertisements
  • Identifies AI-generated content
  • Blocks unwanted AI features

Installation

To install SecondGlance, follow these steps:

  1. Download the extension from the Chrome Web Store or Firefox Add-ons.
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" or "Add to Firefox" and confirm the installation.
  3. Once installed, you can access the settings by clicking on the extension icon in your browser toolbar.

Usage

After installing SecondGlance, it will automatically start detecting ads and AI-generated content on the websites you visit. You can customize the settings to suit your preferences.
